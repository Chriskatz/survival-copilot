## 1 · Hook
**EN —** When disaster strikes — or you're far beyond any signal — the network is the first thing to go. Survival Co-pilot is an off-grid AI that still answers life-saving questions, with no internet, no cell, and no cloud.

## 2 · How it works
**EN —** People carry ordinary Meshtastic LoRa radios. A base station runs a local AI — the language model and retrieval both on-device through the QVAC SDK — and auto-answers across the mesh, with zero infrastructure.

## 3 · Grounded
**EN —** And it stays grounded. Over LoRa, from a phone with no SIM, a hypothermia question is answered in seconds from a curated survival corpus — while an off-topic question is refused, not guessed.

**ZH —** 而且答案有依據。透過 LoRa、用一支沒有 SIM 的手機,失溫問題能在幾秒內依精選求生語料回覆 —— 而離題的問題則被拒答,不亂猜。
